About

My name is Dries Develtere. I am a urologist in Ypres, Belgium, specialising in robotic surgery for prostate and bladder cancer.

Every video on this platform comes directly from the operating room — unsponsored, unfiltered, and designed to give trainees and practicing surgeons the clearest possible cockpit view of how these procedures are actually performed.

Surgical Vision exists because I believe high-quality surgical education should be accessible to anyone, anywhere, at no cost.
